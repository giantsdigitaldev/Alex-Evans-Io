Problem

WGMA) VXZVDORM_+

Music has always been one of the most personal forms of expression—but writing a song takes skill, time, and creativity. People wanted to give meaningful, emotional gifts but didn't know where to start. Traditional tools for music creation felt either too technical or too generic. We wanted to change that.

Root causes

  • Writing custom lyrics

    Required expert skill and creative time.

  • Producers were expensive

    Templates lacked emotional depth.

  • People craved something unique

    More heartfelt than a playlist or greeting card.

We don't have time to write a song from scratch.

Context & Research

WGMA) VXZVDORM_+

By 2025, personalized digital experiences became the new standard—yet music creation had stayed out of reach for most people. With advances in generative AI, we saw an opportunity to democratize songwriting: to let anyone, regardless of skill, bring their relationship, event, or memory to life through song. A personal song became the new love letter, anniversary card, or wedding gift.

"If you don't have a song that fits the moment, people notice."

We interviewed couples, gift givers, and amateur musicians. Most expressed frustration about not knowing how to start writing lyrics or composing melodies—and just wanted a tool that would capture their emotions authentically.

"I'd use it if it sounded like us, not just AI."

What we heard

  • Emotional precision

    80% of people said they'd love to gift a song if it felt personal.

  • Tone authenticity

    "It should sound like us, not just AI," was a recurring theme.

  • Proof of connection

    Testing showed participants got emotional listening to their generated songs.

  • Experience economy

    Custom content became a differentiator for events and gifts.

  • Marketplace gap

    No dominant platform for "custom song for my occasion" at a reachable price.

Solution

WGMA) VXZVDORM_+

We built Songtuna.com to make songwriting emotional, intuitive, and shareable. Users describe their story—how they met, memories they share, or the feeling they want the song to evoke—and the AI generates custom lyrics, melody, and style that reflect that bond.

Platform Features

  • Story-to-song engine

    Transforms user text prompts into fully written and composed songs—lyrics, melody, and style that reflect the user's story and emotions.

  • Emotional tone control

    Adjust sentiment, genre, and tempo to match the user's vision so the song feels authentically theirs.

  • Instant playback & download

    Users can listen, refine, or share their song instantly—no waiting. Download and gift-ready in one click.

  • Gift-ready experiences

    Personalized pages and downloadable keepsakes for sharing with loved ones—weddings, anniversaries, birthdays.

Songtuna reimagines music creation—making it easy for anyone to turn their love, memories, and milestones into a song that lasts forever.
